Club Officers

ODU Run Club is a student organization at Old Dominion University dedicated to all things running — from cross country to track & field, including distance, sprints, throwing, and jumping! Whether you’re continuing your high school athletic career, trying running for the first time, or just looking to stay in shape, you’re welcome here.

We embrace athletes of all skill levels and interests — from casual joggers to competitive racers. In addition to training and competing together, we host fun social events like potlucks, weekend trail runs, and team meetups throughout the year. No membership experience is required — just bring your passion for movement and a desire to be part of a supportive, active community.
